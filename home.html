About Me

Hi, my name is Suyogya Poudel. I was born in June 22, 2001 in Pokhara, Nepal. I don't remember much from my childhood because I moved to the U.S. when I was only 11. But what I do remember is that ever since I was a kid, I thought I would become a docotor until junior year of high school, when I took my first computer science class. I also took a web development class for a semester in junior year. As I continued to code and learn programming languages, I came to realize that whenever I am coding, I lose track of time. I always thought my job should be something I enjoy and not something I have to suffer through and thus decided to study computer science.
